About Octahealth


Octahealth is on a mission to fix the broken healthcare system of India

A passionate group of IITians, engineers, and doctors who are on a mission to fix the broken healthcare system of India and aim to reduce healthcare expenses as much as possible. We are the first company in India that is introducing the concept of an on-demand group of family doctors available 24*7 and building India's first social network for health & wellness. We are very excited to harness the power of AI, ML , and cloud computing technologies to make our vision of affordable and quality preventive healthcare for our members and their families.
